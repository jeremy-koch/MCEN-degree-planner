ME Catalog

How to use this planner:
  • Drag and drop courses from the catalog on the left into any semester block.
  • Start quickly by selecting a 4-year or 5-year template from the dropdown and clicking "Apply".
  • Edit details: Click the pencil (✏️) on any placed course to update its title, credits, or requirement category.
  • Check prerequisites: Courses will highlight in red with a warning if they are placed before their required prerequisites or corequisites.
